Rules

  • 1. There are 11 levels in the hunt. The more you do, better is your rank.

  • 2. The hunt is timed. In case of a rank clash, person who solved earlier would be assigned better rank.

  • 3. Hints will be released to all rounds at an interval of 15 minutes on the Leaderboard page itself.

  • 4. This is an INDIVIDUAL event. Discussion and solving in groups is not allowed.

  • 5. Do NOT share your Registration Number with anyone. It is your password to participate in the event.

  • 6. Don't waste time trying to hack website or stuff. We would know it.

  • 7. If you try to jump a level, you would be instantly disqualified.

  • 8. Prizes would be announced later but they are going to be awesome. You can count on us ;)

  • 9. The hunt will start at 8:00 PM on 13th March, 2022 and will end at 11:00 PM on 13th March, 2022 (IST).
